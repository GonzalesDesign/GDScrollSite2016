Horizon Blue Cross Blue Shield Annual Report

Health Insurance Company

Hired as an Art Director by GraficaGroup. My main account was Horizon Blue Cross Blue Shield of New Jersey and tasked to re-design the site. I worked mostly on the interface design and the standardization of the different graphic elements that went on to the new site. I also built the Sections Control Panel in Flash, it sits right in the middle of all the main sections landing pages. It controls most of the major navigations of the site.
